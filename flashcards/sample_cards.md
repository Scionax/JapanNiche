# Numbers
- ichi: One. [ee-chee] [いち]
- ni: Two. [nee] [に]
- san: Three. [sahn] [さん]

# Particles
- wa: Topic marker. [wah] [は]
- ga: Subject marker. [gah] [が]
- wo: Direct object marker. [oh] [を]

# Expressions
- tanoshii: Fun, enjoyable. [tah-noh-shee-ee] [たのしい]
- omoshiroi: Interesting, funny. [oh-moh-shee-roh-ee] [おもしろい]
